--- layout: default title: Long Term Service Changes ---

The following changes are long term service changes effective until a certain date. This is subject to change on any given day and the directions page for the specific event in question should be checked. In addition, the official MTA website has the latest service changes.

  • No service between Court Sq and Nassau Av


    All times from 10:30 PM Friday, July 25, until 5:00 AM Tuesday, September 2, 2014

    Take free shuttle buses instead.

    Free shuttle buses run along two routes.
    1. Between Court Sq ( weekdays) and Nassau Av via Manhattan Av.
    2. Between Court Sq ( weekdays) and Lorimer St /Metropolitan Av via McGuinness Blvd.
    In addition to transfers to Manhattan-bound trains, a free out-of-system transfer with a MetroCard is available between the Broadway and Lorimer St stations.

  • No service between Whitehall St and Court St


    Weekdays until October 2014

    Service is split into two sections:

    1. Between Forest Hills–71 Av and Whitehall St
    2. Between Court St and Bay Ridge–95 St
    For through service between Manhattan and Brooklyn, transfer between and trains at Canal St and Atlantic Av–Barclays Center.
