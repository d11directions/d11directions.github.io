--- layout: default title: Long Term Service Changes ---

The following changes are long term service changes effective until a certain date. This is subject to change on any given day and the directions page for the specific event in question should be checked. In addition, the official MTA website has the latest service changes.

  • Trains will not stop at Buhre Av and Zerega Av in both directions.

    All times until February 2015

    Local busses provide connecting service.

    Buhre Av:
    Use the Middletown Rd station instead. The Bx8 and Bx24 buses provide service between Middletown Rd and Buhre Av.
    Zerega Av:
    Use the Castle Hill Av station instead. The Bx4 bus provides service between Castle Hill Av and Zerega Av.

    During rush hours and late nights, free shuttle buses run between Buhre Av and Castle Hill Av , making stops at Middletown Rd , Westchester Sq–E Tremont Av , and Zerega Av.
