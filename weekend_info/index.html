The following changes are what typically exist on weekends. This is subject to change on any given weekend and the page for the specific event in question should be checked. In addition, the official MTA website has the latest service changes.

Suspended Service:

  • service does not run.
    • Take local service instead.
  • service does not run.
    • Take local service instead.
  • service does not run.
    • Take service as an alternative in The Bronx or in Manhattan between 59 St–Columbus Circle and Grand St.
    • Take service as an alternative in Manhattan between 145 St and 59 St–Columbus Circle.
    • Take service as an alternative in Brooklyn.
  • service does not run.
    • Take service instead.

Shortened Service:

  • service only runs between Eastchester–Dyre Av and Bowling Green.
    • Transfer at Bowling Green to service to continue your trip.
    • Take service as an alternative between Bowling Green and Franklin Av.
    • Take service as an alternative between Franklin Av and Flatbush Av–Brooklyn College.
  • service only runs between Jamaica Center–Parsons/Archer and Chambers St.
    • Transfer at Chambers St to service to continue your trip.
    • For Fulton St , use Fulton St .
    • For Broad St , use Wall St .
  • service only runs between Middle Village–Metropolitan Av and Essex St/Delancey St.
    • Transfer at Essex St/Delancey St to service to continue your trip.
    • Take service as an alternative in Manhattan.
    • Take service as an alternative in Queens.
  • service only runs between 57 St–7 Av and Coney Island–Stillwell Av.
    • Take service as an alternative between Astoria–Ditmars Blvd and 57 St–7 Av.
